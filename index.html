Car Parking Calculator

This calculator compares parking requirements under the old standards (PPT Network zones) and new standards (4-category system) to help you determine the minimum requirement.

Old Standards (PPT Network)

PPT Network Area applies if:

  • Any part of the land is identified as being within the Principal Public Transport Network Area as shown on the Principal Public Transport Network Area Maps (State Government of Victoria, August 2018).
  • A schedule to the Parking Overlay or another provision of the planning scheme specifies that the PPT Network Area applies.

New Standards (CPR Categories)

Based on the Car Parking Requirement Maps (Department of Transport and Planning, 2025). To find which category your development is in, go to https://mapshare.vic.gov.au/vicplan/ and turn on the "Car Parking Requirement Maps":

  • Category 1: Minimum requirement only
  • Category 2: Minimum requirement only
  • Category 3: Both minimum AND maximum requirements
  • Category 4: Maximum requirement only

If land is in multiple categories, the higher category number applies.
